About

I work where business processes, data, and automation meet, figuring out how organisations can do more with the tools they already have.

As Assistant Manager in MINDEF's Digital Corporate Transformation Division (Enterprise Agility), I redesign workflows, hunt down high-impact automation and productivity use cases, and help teams adopt standardised platforms and GenAI tools across a federated IT environment.

The thread through my career has stayed the same since my first internship: take a messy, manual process and make it measurably better. That's meant flagging financial-crime risk with Python at NETS, automating data entry with VBA at Agilent, or supporting healthtech rollouts at Synapxe.
